YHA - Central London


Location: 104 Bolsover Street City of Westminster W1W 5NU London, UK
Website: YHA - Central London

Rated London’s number one hostel in 2008, 2009 and 2010, YHA London Central boasts seven floors of modern, budget accommodation just five minutes’ walk from Oxford Street - the perfect place for young backpackers wanting to experience the heart of the capital.

This comfortable London central hostel is within easy reach of Oxford Circus, Soho and the West End so there’s great shopping, restaurants and entertainment nearby, and the on-site café/bar is perfect for a relaxing drink, snack and to swap stories with fellow travellers. Facilities at the hostel include a self-catering kitchen, laundry, TV room, Wi-Fi and Internet access.

YHA London Central offer discounts on major attractions including Madame Tussauds, Tower of London and the London Eye, and hostel staff are available to offer local advice and suggestions for visiting some of London’s quirkier corners. Also don’t miss out on the free maps and free daily walking tour.
