Best Western - The Cromwell


Location:110 Cromwell Road, London, SW7 4ES
Website: Best Western - The Cromwell

BEST WESTERN - The Cromwell is located on the Cromwell Road, close to South Kensington. In this quiet location at the heart of the capital, you'll still be within easy reach of London’s best bits. Gloucester Road Tube station is just a few minute’s walk away to make things even easier! From your base, in this distinctive and rather grand white Victorian building, you'll also be close to the areas many museums; perfect for families and couples alike!

Located just moments from the Science Museum, Natural History Museum and the world-famous Victoria & Albert Museum, as well as the department stores Harrods and Harvey Nichols, you'll have enough on your doorstep to keep everybody happy!
