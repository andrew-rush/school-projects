Days Inn - Hyde Park


Location: 148-152 Sussex Gardens, London, W2 1UD, UK
Website: Days Inn - Hyde Park

Paddington station is only 300 yards away, this hotel is ideally located for the Heathrow Express from Paddington (which takes only 15 minutes), and most of the Local attractions are only a quick journey away by bus or underground like Hyde Park - Madame Tussauds - Kensington Palace and Garden etc.

Nearby facilities also include; Edgware Road is about 500 metres away from the hotel, and popular tourist host spots like Marble Arch and Oxford Street are within a kilometre from the hotel's doorstep. You can take a short taxi ride through the picturesque Hyde Park to reach South Kensington, home for the Royal Albert Hall, Earls Court and the Natural History Museum.

The West End is under fifteen minutes by tube or taxi from the hotel, and there are four nearby tube stations within the proximity of the hotel providing simple, convenient transport links. Leicester Square, Covent Garden and Trafalgar Square can be reached in about ten to fifteen minutes.
